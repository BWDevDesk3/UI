Our Service

Whether you seek technical support or want to make your voice heard, We gladly extend our support to you. Smile! help is only few clicks away.

How it works

DevDesk is a platform that allow Lambda students to seek help solving any issue they might encounter. Once the student submits a help ticket, our dedicated team are instantly notified at which point a team member will be assigned to the case, and will either resolve the issue or direct it to the right party to solve it.

View Open tickets

Sign in to get access to your own dashboard, where you can view and manage your submitted help tickets. You can edit, delete, and classify your tickets. You can even pull your open tickets, edit them and push them back.

Manage tickets

Sign in to assign tickets to your account and take action. Mark tickets as "Resolved" if the issue is successfully resolved. Re-enlist the ticket back in the help channel if unable to resolve the issue.
